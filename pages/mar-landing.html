Publications

  • I was the antifouling expert advisor to the engineers on the Ocean Cleanup Project. I authored chapter 5.3, "Prevention and fighting of biofouling" in their 2014 Feasibility study. Here's their Ted Talk, you should watch it!
  • My research has been published in academic journals:
    • Warming off southwestern Japan linked to distributional shifts of subtidal canopy-forming seaweeds
    • The relative magnitude of the effects of biological and physical settlement cues for cypris larvae of the acorn barnacle
    • Field-based video observations of wild barnacle cyprid behaviour in response to textural and chemical settlement cues
    • A comparative study of the anti-settlement properties of mytilid shells
  • I was the invited author of a chapter entitled Settlement and Behaviour of Marine Fouling Organisms in the textbook "Biofouling",
  • and the 100 page "Open Ocean" section of an online marine biology course currently offered by Newcastle University, entitled Delve Deeper
  • I have peer reviewed several submissions for publication with Biofouling - The Journal of Bioadhesion and Biofilm Research and The Canadian Journal of Zoology.
